The Stevens ID Card is the official photo identification card at Stevens Institute of Technology. Not only is it your ID, but it also gives you access to DuckBills, your meal plan, door access and more. Your Stevens ID Card is valid as long as you are enrolled at Stevens or if you are an active employee of the University.  Students, staff, faculty and guests should always carry their Stevens ID.
